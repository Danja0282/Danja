Mission

CEHED is out to improve and enrich the lives of people with emphasis on youth and women purposively through sensitization, advocacy programmes, climate smart farming, sustainable, eco-friendly environmental sensitization, sanitation awareness, vocational, entrepreneurship trainings, and youth development programmes.

Vision

Our vision is to see people especially youth and women have access to basic education, health, entrepreneurship skills and opportunities for better life.
